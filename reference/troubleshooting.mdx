Workspace and dispatch problems live in [Troubleshooting workspaces](/reference/troubleshooting-workspaces).

## Sign-in

**"Authentication failed." when signing in**
Check your network and retry. If you just created the account, confirm your email first ("Check your email to confirm your account before signing in.") and use **Resend confirmation email** if needed.

**Confirmation email never arrives**
Check spam, wait a minute, then **Resend confirmation email**. Links expire after 24 hours; request a fresh one if yours is old.

**Signed out unexpectedly**
Long-expired sessions require a fresh sign-in. Local workspaces are unaffected.

## Provider CLIs

**Provider card stuck on "Not installed"**
Click **Install CLI** and let Syndicate install it with its bundled runtime. If it fails ("Install failed."), open the diagnostic modal, use **Copy diagnostic**, and send it to support.

**"CLI installed — click Connect to sign in" but Connect seems to do nothing**
Look for an opened browser window or terminal behind the app. The sign-in continues there; Syndicate auto-detects completion.

## Claude

**Pasted code rejected ("Invalid code — try again.")**
Codes expire in about 15 minutes. Restart Connect and paste the new code promptly.

**"Claude OAuth endpoint is busy — switching to terminal login."**
Not an error: the automatic fallback. Finish signing in inside the terminal window; the card flips to Connected on its own.

## OpenAI (Codex)

**"This ChatGPT account cannot run Codex."**
Your ChatGPT plan does not include Codex. Upgrade the plan or use Claude or Gemini instead.

**Signed in but card stays "Not connected"**
Click **Check** to re-probe. If it persists, **Reconnect** and complete the terminal flow again without closing the terminal early.

## Gemini

**Terminal opens but sign-in stalls**
Complete the Google flow, including two-factor prompts. On macOS the flow uses Terminal.app; bring it to front. If the terminal exited early ("Auth terminal exited with code X before completion."), close and Connect again.

## Mid-session

**Amber banner: provider credentials expired**
Click **Reconnect [Provider]** in the banner, finish the sign-in, then re-prompt any stopped agents. Queued work is preserved.

**Models missing from selectors**
Model lists only include authenticated providers. Check Settings, Providers: the provider must show **Connected** and a non-empty Models row. **Refresh** forces a re-check.

## Still stuck?

Send details through the in-app feedback form (paste screenshots directly) or email [support@usesyndicate.org](mailto:support@usesyndicate.org). Include the diagnostic copy if an install failed.
