Syndicate is in private beta. Request an invite, install the app, and sign in.

{/* Hero media: 15-20 second clip — invite email, installer download, first launch, account creation, signed-in app. */}

## Request beta access

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open usesyndicate.org">
    Go to [usesyndicate.org](https://usesyndicate.org) and click **Request Access**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit your email">
    Enter the address you want to use for your Syndicate account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Watch for the invite">
    The invite email includes macOS and Windows installer links.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Install Syndicate

{/* Screenshot: beta invite email with macOS/Windows download buttons. */}

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install the app">
    From the invite email, download the installer for your OS and run it. Approve any prompts from your OS.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Launch Syndicate">
    Open the desktop app and click **Get Started**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Choose **Create account** and enter the email you used for beta access.

    <Frame>
      ![Create your Syndicate account form with fields for full name, email, password, and confirm password.](/assets/screenshots/install/create_account.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm your email">
    Confirm your email if Syndicate prompts you.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign in">
    Sign in with your new account. If Syndicate says the account is already active elsewhere, sign in here to take over the session.

    <Frame>
      ![Syndicate sign-in screen with email and password fields and a Create account link.](/assets/screenshots/install/sign_in.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
If the confirmation email does not arrive, use **Resend confirmation email** in the app and check spam.
</Tip>

## Done when

- Syndicate opens and you are signed in.
- The app prompts you to connect at least one [provider](/features/providers-and-models).
