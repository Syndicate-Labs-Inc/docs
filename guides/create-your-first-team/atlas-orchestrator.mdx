## What this guide will cover

This page will explain how Atlas helps you:

- Turn an outcome into a plan.
- Delegate work to the right specialist.
- Track task progress across the team.
- Route follow-up messages and blockers.
- Ask for approval based on the team's control mode.
- Summarize progress before you decide what to do next.

## Current guidance

Until this guide is complete, use Atlas the same way you use the Manager in a team:

1. Give Atlas a clear outcome.
2. Add constraints that matter, such as files to avoid or approval requirements.
3. Review the plan or delegation before work expands.
4. Watch tasks and inbox messages for blockers.
5. Reply with one concrete next instruction when Atlas asks for input.
