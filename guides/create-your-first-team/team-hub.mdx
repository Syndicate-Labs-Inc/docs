The Team Hub is the landing grid for every team you have. Use it to see at a glance which teams need you, which are working, and which are idle.

## Reading a team card

Each card shows the team name, agent count, and task progress, plus a status badge:

| Badge | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Needs Input** / **Awaiting Review** (yellow) | Agents are blocked or waiting on your decision. Open the team and check User Tasks. |
| **In Progress** / **Running** (green) | Agents are actively working. |
| **Quiet** (gray) | There is history but everyone is idle. |
| **New** / **Idle** (gray) | Nothing happening yet. |

Counters along the bottom show active agents (green dot), errors (red dot), and total unread messages (envelope icon).

## Card actions

The **More actions** menu on each card:

- **Project Settings**: rename, relink the folder, interruption level, pause, delete.
- **Pause Team** / **Resume Team**: hard-pause toggles directly from the hub.
- **Add Agent**: drop a new specialist into the team.
- **Delete Team**: permanent removal (project files on disk are kept).

Click the card or **Open team** to enter the [team dashboard](/features/teams#the-team-dashboard).

## Solo agents

Solo workspaces appear under their own **Solo Agents** section in the sidebar, with the same status dots. See [Solo agents](/features/solo-agents).

## Empty state

A fresh install shows "No teams yet. Create your first team to get started with orchestration." with a **New Team** button, which opens the [creation wizard](/guides/create-your-first-team/quick-start).
