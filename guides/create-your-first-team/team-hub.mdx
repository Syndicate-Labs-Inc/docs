**Team Hub** is the landing view for multi-agent work. Open it when you want to scan teams before entering a specific **Team Workspace**.

## When to use Team Hub

Use **Team Hub** when you want to:

- See every team in one place.
- Check team status and unread updates.
- Reopen a team you used recently.
- Create a new team without staying inside another workspace.
- Open team settings before you enter the workspace.

If you already know which workspace you need, use the sidebar. Use **Team Hub** when you want the broader team view.

## What team cards show

Each team card can show:

- Team name.
- Working folder label.
- Agent count and roster summary.
- Agent avatars or status summary.
- Team status based on agent status.
- Unread or recent activity signals.
- Settings access.
- An open or drill-in action.

A team appears active when agents are working. Waiting, error, idle, and hard-paused states also affect the summary.

## Focused team preview

When you select a team in **Team Hub**, the focused preview can show:

- Team name.
- Working folder label.
- Agent roster.
- Active status.
- A primary action to open the team chat or workspace.
- Team settings access.
- Inbox messages scoped to that team.

Open the team when you need the full **Team Workspace** with chat, **Agent Comms**, **User Tasks**, **Agent Tasks**, **Task Health**, and the bottom tray.

## Create a team from Team Hub

Click **Create Team** to start the same setup flow covered in [Quick start](/guides/create-your-first-team/quick-start). Choose **Template**, **Guided description**, or **Build Your Team**; select the working folder; review Atlas and specialists; choose **Interruption Level**; and create the team.

## Jump back into recent work

Open a team from **Team Hub** when you want to continue where you left off. After **Team Workspace** opens, check:

- The latest Atlas message.
- **User Tasks** for decisions, approvals, or blockers.
- **Agent Tasks** for tracked work.
- **Task Health** for risk signals.
- **Agent Comms** for specialist handoffs.
- **Activity**, **Files**, and **Uploads** in the bottom tray.
