Once a team is running, your job is mostly triage: answer what needs you, watch the board, and keep Atlas honest. Reference detail lives in [Tasks](/features/tasks) and [Inbox and Agent Comms](/features/inbox).

## The five-minute check-in

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check the bell">
    The inbox bell in the title bar shows your unread count across all workspaces. Click it for the latest messages; anything pending has a blue dot.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear User Tasks">
    Open the team and work through **User Tasks** top to bottom: **Reply** to questions (Atlas relays your answer to the right agent), **Resolve** anything already handled. Items marked **Blocking** are stopping work right now.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Scan Agent Tasks">
    Glance at the board: are the in-progress tasks the ones you expect? **Cancel** anything stale or off-goal. If the whole direction is wrong, **Abort all** and tell Atlas the new plan.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Glance at Task Health">
    If items pile up here, **Sweep stale tasks** or **Nudge Manager** on specific entries. A clean board reads "Atlas is keeping the board clean."
  </Step>
  <Step title="Ask for a brief">
    In Atlas chat, fire a Quick Brief: **Status brief**, **Blockers**, or **Needs from me**, instead of scrolling history.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Assigning work directly

Use **Assign task** (slash menu in Atlas chat, or the Assign Task form): pick the agent, describe the work, and choose **Notify** (inbox), **Start** (immediately), or **Flag** (needs your decision first). Check **Requires human decision** for work that must come back to you before it lands.

## Replying from the inbox

The full inbox panel splits **Received** and **Sent**. On a received message use **Reply** for an inline answer or **Forward to chat** to move the thread into the agent's chat where you can attach files and steer.

## Habits that keep teams healthy

- Answer **Blocking** items quickly; everything downstream of them is stalled.
- Cancel ruthlessly. A short board Atlas can reason about beats a long aspirational one.
- If updates feel noisy in Atlas chat, uncheck **Show updates from agents**; the information is still in Agent Comms and the Activity tray.
