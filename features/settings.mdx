Open Settings from the sidebar account footer, the command palette, or a team's gear menu. Four tabs.

## Account

- **Profile**: your avatar, display name, email, and tier badge (free, beta, pro, gold), plus your **Team limit** and **Solo limit**.
- **Sign out**: clears your session on this machine.
- **Subscription**: "Billing and plan management arrives in Phase 2." The Manage subscription button is not active yet.

## General

- **Theme**: **Light** or **Dark**. Applies immediately and persists.
- **Confine agents to their working folder**: the workspace confinement toggle. "Runs agents inside the OS sandbox so they stay fully autonomous but can't read or write files outside their team's working folder." Also stops macOS permission prompts for personal folders. Network access is unaffected. Not available on Windows (Windows does not prompt for folder access). Details: [Permissions and safety](/reference/permissions-and-safety).
- **syndicate version**: your current version, a **Check for updates** button, and a **Restart now** button when an update has downloaded. See [Data, storage, and updates](/reference/data-and-storage#updates).

## Providers

- **Default Model For New Agents**: provider and model dropdowns. "Used for new teams and solo agents. Existing agents keep their current model."
- **Provider Health**: a card per provider (Claude, OpenAI, Google Gemini) with its **Connected** / **Not connected** badge, CLI version, model list, and context-dependent actions: **Install CLI**, **Connect**, **Reconnect**, **Disconnect**, **Refresh**. Claude sign-in may show a code-paste form here. Full flows: [Connect providers](/guides/connect-providers).

## MCP

- **Installed servers**: every MCP server with health dot, transport badge, **Test**, enable/disable toggle, and **Remove**.
- **Add a server**: manual install by local command, remote URL, or SSE, with encrypted secrets.
- **Catalog**: one-click presets for common servers.

Full guide: [MCP servers](/features/mcp).

A consolidated list is in the [settings reference](/reference/settings).
