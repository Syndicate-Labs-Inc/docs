Agents coordinate through inbox messages. In Team Workspace, you usually read that coordination in **Agent Comms**.

## What inbox messages are for

Agents use inbox messages to:

- Delegate work.
- Ask another specialist to review something.
- Report completion.
- Request clarification.
- Escalate a user decision to Atlas.

## Agent Comms

**Agent Comms** is the user-facing view of inter-agent dispatch messages.

For a selected specialist, it shows messages received from Atlas or other agents and messages sent by that specialist. For Atlas, it shows the broader team dispatch stream.

Use **Agent Comms** to inspect coordination without cluttering your direct chat with the selected agent.

## Unread counts

Unread badges show which agents or teams have new coordination activity. **Team Hub** can also surface unread and recent messages across teams or for the focused team.

Click an unread item to open the related team and agent. When the item needs your response, Syndicate can route it into **User Tasks**.

## User attention

When an agent needs your input, it can route the request through Atlas or surface it as a **User Task**. Treat these items as decision points. The team may soft pause while it waits for your answer.

Use **Reply** to answer Atlas, **Resolve** to close an item without a reply, or **Allow** / **Dismiss** for interruption prompts.

## Clearing confusion

If messages pile up or the team seems stuck:

1. Open **User Tasks** and resolve anything blocking.
2. Open **Agent Comms** for Atlas and the relevant specialist.
3. Check **Task Health** for blocked or risky work.
4. Stop any agent doing the wrong work.
5. Give Atlas one clear next instruction.
