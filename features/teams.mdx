A team is a Manager (Atlas) plus specialist agents sharing one working directory, one task board, and one message bus.

## Finding and switching teams

The sidebar is the team navigator. The **Team Hub** accordion lists every team; click one to open its workspace, or expand it to jump straight to a specific agent.

Each team row carries a live indicator, prioritized by what needs you most:

- **Amber pulsing dot + count**: agents waiting on you
- **Red dot + count**: agents blocked by errors
- **Green dot + count**: agents actively working
- **Gray count**: idle (shows the agent count)

When you launch the app with existing teams, Syndicate auto-opens your most recently active team. Clicking the Syndicate logo returns you to the welcome page.

## The team dashboard

Inside a team you get three tabs:

- **Dashboard**: the visual canvas plus task panels (described below).
- **Chat**: your direct conversation with Atlas.
- **Dispatch**: the raw feed of agent-to-agent messages.

### The canvas

Atlas sits at the center with agent tiles around it. Animated lines glow when messages flow between agents. Each tile shows the agent's name, its model provider icon, and a status dot:

| Dot | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Green | Active: currently working |
| Gray | Idle |
| Amber | Waiting: needs approval or input |
| Red | Blocked: hit an error |

Below each tile: task progress ("1/5 tasks"), files modified, and the agent's latest summary. Click any tile to open that agent's chat.

### The task panels

The right column has three panels. The full breakdown is in [Tasks](/features/tasks):

- **User Tasks**: everything waiting on you. Reply or resolve each item.
- **Agent Tasks**: the work board Atlas manages. You can cancel individual tasks or **Abort all**.
- **Task Health**: board hygiene issues (stale, unowned, unclear tasks). Use **Nudge Manager** or **Sweep stale tasks** to have Atlas clean up.

### The bottom tray

A collapsible tray with **Files** (everything agents created or modified, with previews) and **Activity** (a chronological log of commands run and files changed, with **Load more** for older history).

## Team controls

Open **Team Settings** (gear icon or card menu) to manage:

- **Project Name**: rename the team.
- **Working Directory**: browse to relink the team to a different folder. Agents and history are kept.
- **Interruption Level** (teams with a Manager): how often Atlas stops to ask you. Four modes: **Minimal**, **Balanced**, **Hands-on**, **Manual**. See [Working with Atlas](/guides/create-your-first-team/atlas-orchestrator#interruption-level).
- **Team Hard Pause**: stops current dispatches and blocks auto-dispatch. While paused, the chat input is disabled with "Team paused. Resume the team before prompting agents." Resuming does not restart agents; they only run when prompted again.
- **Danger Zone**: **Delete Team** permanently removes the team and all its agents (two-step confirmation). Project files on disk are untouched.

There is also a **Stop team** button in the workspace header for an immediate stop of the current run, separate from the hard pause.

## Team limits

Your account tier sets how many teams and solo workspaces you can create, and the maximum agents per team. Your current limits are shown in Settings under **Account**.
