## What you can do with Syndicate

- Create AI teams that share a working folder and project context.
- Add specialized agents for engineering, design, QA, research, documentation, and more.
- Connect multiple AI providers and choose the right model for each agent.
- Use skills, MCP servers, and local tools to extend what agents can do.
- Keep work organized with team files, task lists, and agent-specific instructions.
