Provider setup connects Syndicate to the model accounts your agents use.

## Open provider settings

Go to **Settings > Providers**.

## Connect a provider

Choose Claude, OpenAI/Codex, or Gemini and click **Connect**.

## Complete authentication

Depending on the provider, Syndicate may open a browser sign-in flow or show a terminal-assisted flow.

## Verify readiness

A provider is ready when it is installed, authenticated, and has available models.

## Common things to notice

- Codex can be signed in but still fail capability verification.
- Gemini may need visible terminal interaction.
- Claude prefers browser OAuth, with terminal fallback only when needed.
