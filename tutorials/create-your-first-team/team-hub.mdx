## When to open Team hub

Open Team hub when you want to:

- See every team you have at a glance.
- Check which teams have providers connected and which are blocked.
- Jump back into a team you have not opened recently.
- Create a new team without leaving a workspace mid-run.

If you already know which workspace you want, the sidebar is faster. Use Team hub when you want the cross-team view.

## What you see on the landing page

- **Your teams** — every multi-agent workspace, with the most recently active first.
- **Provider state** — which teams have a working provider connected and which need attention.
- **Recent activity** — the teams that have run work most recently, so you can pick up where you left off.
- **Create Team** — entry point into the team creation flow.

## Create a new team from the hub

Use the **Create Team** entry on the hub when you are starting fresh. The flow is the same one covered in [Quick start](/tutorials/create-your-first-team) — pick manual or guided setup, choose a project folder, review agents, and finish.
