The team view is the workspace you land in after picking a team from the sidebar or [Team hub](/tutorials/create-your-first-team/team-hub). Everything a team does — chat, dispatch, tasks, inboxes, and references — lives here.

## Layout at a glance

A team workspace usually shows:

- **Sidebar** — the Manager and specialists on the team, with status indicators.
- **Chat** — where you message the Manager (or a specialist directly) and watch dispatches happen.
- **Tasks** — the work the Manager has broken out and assigned.
- **Inbox** — messages waiting for your attention from agents on the team.

The exact placement may shift as the app evolves, but the four areas above are the ones to learn first.

## What each area is for

| Area | Use it to |
| --- | --- |
| Sidebar | See who is on the team and who is active right now. |
| Chat | Give the Manager an outcome, watch dispatches, and reply when an agent asks. |
| Tasks | Track what is in flight, what is done, and what is blocked. |
| Inbox | Catch up on agent messages without scrubbing through chat. |

For deeper coverage of these surfaces, see [chat and dispatch](/features/chat-and-dispatch), [tasks](/features/tasks), and [inbox](/features/inbox).

## First moves in a new team

After [creating your first team](/tutorials/create-your-first-team):

1. Open the team from the sidebar or Team hub.
2. Send the Manager one outcome — for example, "summarize this project."
3. Watch the Manager dispatch to a specialist.
4. Check the inbox when the specialist reports back.

<Tip>
Keep the first task small. It's the fastest way to learn how dispatches, the inbox, and tasks fit together.
</Tip>
