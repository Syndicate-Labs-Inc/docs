Use this tutorial when you are installing Syndicate on a new machine.

## Download the app

1. Go to the Syndicate website.
2. Download the installer for your operating system.
3. Run the installer.
4. Open Syndicate.

## Open Settings

If Syndicate asks you to connect a provider, open Settings and choose the provider you want to use first.

## Confirm the app is ready

Syndicate is ready when:

- The app opens without a blocking setup screen.
- At least one provider shows as connected.
- You can create a team or open an existing team.
