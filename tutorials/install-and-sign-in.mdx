## Request access

{/* Screenshot: usesyndicate.org with Request Access visible. */}

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the website">
    Go to [usesyndicate.org](https://usesyndicate.org).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Request beta access">
    Click **Request Access** and submit the email address you want to use with Syndicate.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for approval">
    The beta invite email is what gives you the correct installer path.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Install Syndicate

{/* Screenshot: beta invite email with Windows/Mac download buttons. */}
{/* Video: download installer, run installer, open Syndicate. */}

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your beta invite">
    Use the invite sent to your approved email address.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download the app">
    Choose the installer for your operating system.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run the installer">
    Follow the operating system prompts and approve the app if asked.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open Syndicate">
    Launch the desktop app after installation finishes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Create your account

{/* Screenshot: Get Started screen. */}
{/* Screenshot: Create account form. */}

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start from the welcome screen">
    Click **Get Started**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Choose **Create account** and use the same email address approved for beta access.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm your email">
    Complete email confirmation if Syndicate prompts you to do it.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
If the confirmation email does not arrive, use **Resend confirmation email** in the app and check spam.
</Tip>

## Sign in on another machine

{/* Screenshot: device takeover or active session message, if available. */}

1. Install Syndicate on the new machine.
2. Click **Get Started**.
3. Sign in with your approved account.
4. If Syndicate says the account is active on another computer, sign in again on this machine to take over.

## Done when

- Syndicate opens successfully.
- You can sign in with your approved beta account.
- The app prompts you to connect at least one AI provider.
