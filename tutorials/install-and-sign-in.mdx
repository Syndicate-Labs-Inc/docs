Syndicate is currently in private beta. You need an approved email address before you can create an account.

## Request access

1. Go to [usesyndicate.org](https://usesyndicate.org).
2. Click **Request Access**.
3. Submit the email address you want to use with Syndicate.
4. Wait for the beta approval email.

## Install Syndicate

After approval, your invite email includes download links.

1. Open the beta invite email.
2. Download the installer for your operating system.
3. Run the installer.
4. Open Syndicate.
5. If your operating system asks for permission to run the app, approve it.

## Create your account

1. Open Syndicate.
2. Click **Get Started**.
3. Choose **Create account**.
4. Use the same email address that was approved for beta access.
5. Confirm your email if prompted.

<Tip>
If the confirmation email does not arrive, use **Resend confirmation email** in the app and check spam.
</Tip>

## Sign in on another machine

1. Install Syndicate on the new machine.
2. Click **Get Started**.
3. Sign in with your approved account.
4. If Syndicate says the account is active on another computer, sign in again on this machine to take over.

## What happens next

After you sign in, Syndicate asks you to connect at least one AI provider before creating your first workspace.
