Syndicate is in private beta. Request access with the email you want to use, then install the app and sign in.

{/* Hero media: 15-20 second clip — invite email, installer download, first launch, account creation, signed-in app. */}

## Request beta access

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open usesyndicate.org">
    Go to [usesyndicate.org](https://usesyndicate.org) and click **Request Access**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit your email">
    Use the address you want as your Syndicate account email.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Watch for the invite">
    The beta invite email contains the macOS and Windows installer download links.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Install and sign in

{/* Screenshot: beta invite email with macOS/Windows download buttons. */}

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install the app">
    Open your beta invite, download the installer for your operating system, and run it. Approve the app if your OS prompts you.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Launch Syndicate">
    Open the desktop app and click **Get Started** on the welcome screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Choose **Create account** and use the same email approved for beta access.

    <Frame>
      ![Create your Syndicate account form with fields for full name, email, password, and confirm password.](/assets/screenshots/install/create_account.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm your email">
    Complete email confirmation if Syndicate prompts you.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign in">
    Sign in with your new account. If Syndicate says the account is active on another computer, sign in again here to take over the session.

    <Frame>
      ![Syndicate sign-in screen with email and password fields and a Create account link.](/assets/screenshots/install/sign_in.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
If the confirmation email does not arrive, use **Resend confirmation email** in the app and check spam.
</Tip>

## Done when

- Syndicate opens and you are signed in.
- The app prompts you to connect at least one AI provider.
